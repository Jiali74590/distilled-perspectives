# Peter Lynch - Writings 调研

## 三本核心著作

### 1. One Up on Wall Street (1989)
- 合著者：John Rothchild
- 销量：100万+册
- 出版社：Simon & Schuster
- 发布时机：1989年，正是他管理Magellan的最后一年

**核心论点**：
- **业余投资者有优势**：普通人通过日常观察（工作、购物、家庭消费）能发现Wall Street专业分析师还没注意到的好公司。"普通人用3%的脑力就能跟专业机构比拼"
- **"Invest in what you know"**：这是贯穿全书的核心——但必须强调，观察只是起点（lead），研究才是信念（conviction）的来源
- **六类股票分类系统**（核心框架）：
  1. **Slow Growers**（缓慢增长股）：行业成熟、靠分红、增长<10%。通常避开
  2. **Stalwarts**（稳健增长股）：大公司，年增长10-12%，Coca-Cola、P&G类型。危机时可买，40-50%涨幅就兑现
  3. **Fast Growers**（快速增长股）：小型高增长（20-25%），Lynch最爱，tenbagger主要来源
  4. **Cyclicals**（周期股）：汽车、航空、钢铁。时机最重要，错过时机就是灾难
  5. **Turnarounds**（困境反转股）：Chrysler最经典案例
  6. **Asset Plays**（资产股）：市场看不到的隐藏价值（土地、品牌、专利）

**Tenbagger 概念**：
- 定义：涨10倍的股票
- 出处：来自棒球术语"bagger"（一个bag=一垒）
- Lynch说：最容易找tenbagger的地方在家门口——shopping mall、工作场所、家人用的产品

**业余投资者的信息优势**：
- Lynch以妻子Carolyn买L'eggs发现Hanes为经典案例
- 他女儿买Body Shop产品让他注意到这家公司
- "Your edge is not what you read in the Wall Street Journal"

### 2. Beating the Street (1993)
- 合著者：John Rothchild
- 时机：退休3年后出版，总结Magellan经验
- 核心增量：详细的stock-by-stock case study，以及**"25 Golden Rules"**

**25 Golden Rules核心条目**（从多来源整理）：
1. Investing is fun and exciting but dangerous if you don't do any work
2. Your investor's edge is not something you get from Wall Street experts. It's something you already have.
3. Over the past three decades, the stock market has come to be dominated by a herd of professional investors. Contrary to popular belief, this makes it easier for the amateur investor.
4. Behind every stock is a company. Find out what it's doing.
5. Often, there is no correlation between the success of a company's operations and the success of its stock over a few months or even a few years. In the long term, there is a 100 percent correlation between the success of the company and the success of its stock.
6. You have to know what you own, and why you own it. "This baby is a cinch to go up!" doesn't count.
7. Long shots almost always miss the mark.
8. Owning stocks is like having children — don't get involved with more than you can handle.
9. If you can't find any companies that you think are attractive, put your money in the bank until you discover some.
10. Never invest in a company without understanding its finances.
11. Avoid hot stocks in hot industries.
12. With small companies, you're better off to wait until they turn a profit before you invest.
13. If you're considering a stock based on a promising story, make sure the story isn't just a pitch.
14. If you study 10 companies, you'll find 1 for which the story is better than expected. If you study 50, you'll find 5.
15. If you don't study any companies, you have the same success buying stocks as you do in a poker game if you bet without looking at your cards.
16. When you sell in desperation, you always sell cheap.
17. In the long run, a portfolio of well-chosen stocks and/or equity mutual funds will always outperform bonds or a money-market account.
18. Time is on your side when you own shares of superior companies.
19. If you have the stomach for stocks, but neither the time nor the inclination to do the homework, invest in equity mutual funds.
20. Among the major stock markets of the world, the U.S. market ranks ... —skipped in multiple sources
21. Just because a stock goes down doesn't mean it can't go lower.
22. Just because the price goes up doesn't mean you're right.
23. The stocks of mediocre companies can go up just as fast as the stocks of superior ones — but the risk is much greater.
24. Corporate finances ...—various formulations
25. There is always something to worry about.

**关键金句**：
- "The person that turns over the most rocks wins the game."
- "Let your winners run, and cut your losers" + "pulling out the flowers and watering the weeds"（Buffett打电话求授权引用此句）

### 3. Learn to Earn (1995)
- 合著者：John Rothchild
- 目标读者：teenager / 投资入门者
- 重点：
  - 股票不是赌博，是拥有企业的一部分
  - 长期复利的力量
  - 避免债务陷阱
  - 培养兴趣是最重要的
  - 储蓄+投资=财富

## 自创术语（多处反复出现，≥3次）

| 术语 | 含义 | 来源 |
|------|------|------|
| **Tenbagger** | 涨10倍的股票 | One Up（贯穿三本书） |
| **Cocktail party theory** | 用鸡尾酒会话题判断市场见顶 | One Up ch.5 |
| **Invest in what you know** | 用生活经验找投资机会 | 三本书核心论点 |
| **Story stock** | 有清晰故事的股票 | 反复使用 |
| **Whisper stock** | 小道消息股（警惕） | One Up |
| **PEG ratio** | PE/增长率=1为合理 | Lynch普及（非他首创） |
