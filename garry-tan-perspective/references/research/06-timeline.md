# Garry Tan: Complete Timeline

## Overview

Garry Tan (born 1981) -- Canadian-American venture capitalist, engineer, designer, entrepreneur. Currently President & CEO of Y Combinator. Founder of Initialized Capital. Known for early-stage investing, political activism in San Francisco, and content creation.
